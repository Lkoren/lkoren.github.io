Liav Koren // artisanal electrons/handcrafted pixels.

While working as half of the two-man interactive system team at Philip Beesley Architect, I was intimately in the design, deployment, testing and debugging of the embedded electronics for two complex interactive installations. Sargasso was a temporary installation in the Brookfielf Atrium during Luminato in 2011. Hylozoic Veil was a permanent installation in The Leonardo, a new Art/Science museum, in Salt Lake City.



I worked closely with a range of consultants, manufactures, industrial designers, Philip Beesley and a diverse team of volunteers and staff successfully install these sculptures.
