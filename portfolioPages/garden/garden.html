This is a proposal for a small intervention into a downtown Toronto backyard. Like most Toronto houses, the site was narrow and deep, with a mature tree to the south, and chain-link fencing seperating the lots.

The proposal is for a trellis/screening structure and tall grasses planted on the north side of the lot, to provide privacy and a sense of enclosure to the space.
