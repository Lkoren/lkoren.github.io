Liav Koren // Portfolio Version 1.0 // 2008 - 2012

I made the Long-Tall shelf a few years ago as part of an industrial design class, exploring low-weight/high strength techniques. The shelf was intended to be a feature piece of furniture and provide a generious 3m of shelving space.



The construction techniques included a mix of techniques -- the bowed structural element is hand-laminated pine; the legs were CNC milled then hand finished.



This gallery documents the final product as well as some of the design explorations and mock-ups that I produced.
