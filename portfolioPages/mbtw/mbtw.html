Liav Koren // artisanal electrons/handcrafted pixels.

During 2010 and 2011 I was a junior designer at MBTW | Watchorn, a medium sized landscape architecture and architectural design firm. I worked primarily with a small team on the soft and hardscaping of a wide range of projects including several condos, parks and municipal projects. I really enjoyed the faster pace at MBTW, where drawing sets were smaller but I was involved in many more projects than would be typical at an architectural firm. I also really enjoyed the chance to be involved in the design of a large number of site amenities such as custom signage boxes for Pier 27, water features at Fly condo and St Michael's college, custom trellises and a particularly striking fireplace for the Scenic condo on Eglington.
