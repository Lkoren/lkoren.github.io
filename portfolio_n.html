Liav Koren // Portfolio Version 1.0 // 2008 - 2012

This is an archived copy of v1.0 of my portfolio gallery. Inspired by Jason Santa Maria's idea of versioning portfolio work, I've archiving this portfolio gallery largely As It Was. This gallery features work from 2008 - 2012, and captures something of how my professional trajectory evolved from very standard architecture/design work to much more creative technologist style work. It's also interesting what it fails to capture -- any sense of interaction or the code that powered the later, creative technologist work I did with Philip Beesley and Panda Robotics. Both elements which will be much more prominent in v2.0



I have been lucky enough to work on a wide range of projects and at several interesting, innovative and award-winning firms. I have more than four years of architectural and design experience, ranging from prestige commerical tower projects, to small-scale retail, residential and landscape architecture.



In recent years I have been very lucky to have a chance to incorporate my interest in new media, interaction design and embedded hardware into my design practice through work I've done with Philip Beesley Architects and as R&D head at Panda Robotics, a small 3D printer start-up.
